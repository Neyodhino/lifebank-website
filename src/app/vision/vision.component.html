At LifeBank, we believe no African person should die because of the critical supplies they need was not available when and where they needed it.

This is LifeBank!

Busy hospital teams across the developing world need access to critical medical supplies. Broken supply chains often lead to the worst outcome for patients even in the best of times.

In Africa, a robust health supply chain engine must deliver from ports to clinic and do so 24/7. This infrastructure engine must be affordable, flexible, and serve all to the very last mile. This is LifeBank!

Multi-Modal Delivery Network

We deliver critical supplies with bikes, boats, trucks, tricycles, and drones faster, cheaper, and safer!

Our Tech

We build tech tools to organise our distribution system using deep tech like AI and Blockchain, deployed with accessible tools like USSD, and SMS systems to ensure universal access.
